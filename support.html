Frequently Asked Questions

How does BizMentorAI analyze my documents?
BizMentorAI uses on-device AI to extract financial data from scanned invoices, receipts, and reports, calculating key metrics like revenue, expenses, cash flow, and margins.
Is my data stored securely?
Yes — all documents and data are stored locally using Core Data. We prioritize privacy and do not upload or share your information externally.
Can I generate business reports?
Yes — you can generate professional PDF reports summarizing financial health, trends, and AI-based recommendations for improvement.
Do I need a subscription?
The free version provides limited scans and analytics. Upgrade to BizMentorAI Pro for unlimited document processing, AI insights, and advanced reports.
Can I request new features?
Absolutely. We welcome your feedback and ideas to make BizMentorAI even more helpful for your business.
